Interactive Scripture Study Tool - Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: ScriptureConnect

This name reflects the site's purpose to connect users more deeply with scripture through interactive study tools, community discussion, and daily engagement.

Optional domain availability: scriptureconnect.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of ScriptureConnect is to support meaningful and interactive scripture study experiences. The site offers daily study prompts, tools for highlighting and taking notes on verses, a forum to share spiritual insights, and a randomized scripture of the day to inspire users each time they visit.

Scenarios

  • What scripture can I study today to feel more peace and spiritual strength?
  • How can I share my thoughts about a verse with others and see their insights?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #4A7C59 (Earthy Green) – Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Accent Color: #E8DAB2 (Light Sand) – Used for backgrounds and section highlights.

Typography

  • Merriweather – Used for all headings to convey a sense of tradition and reflection.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text for readability and a clean modern look.
